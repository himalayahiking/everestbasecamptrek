About Us

Welcome to Trekking Adventures! We are passionate about guiding adventurers through the breathtaking landscapes of Nepal, Tibet, Bhutan, and beyond. With over 25 years of experience, we offer personalized, expertly crafted trekking journeys that are unforgettable.

Our founder, Bikesh Rana, is an expert trekker with years of personal experience in the Himalayas and other global trekking regions. Having completed the Great Himalaya Trail and led many successful expeditions, Bikesh embodies the spirit of exploration and adventure, making your journey with us truly special.

At Trekking Adventures, our mission is to offer more than just a trek. We create life-changing experiences where you can immerse yourself in nature, culture, and adventure, all while being guided by local experts who understand the terrain, people, and spirit of the Himalayas.

Our Core Values

  • Passion: We are driven by a love for the mountains and adventure.
  • Expertise: 25+ years of experience in leading treks through challenging terrains.
  • Safety: Your safety is our priority, ensuring an enjoyable trek with experienced guides.
  • Sustainability: We promote eco-friendly practices to preserve nature's beauty for future generations.
  • Authenticity: We focus on offering authentic, culturally rich experiences in every destination.
